Frequently Asked Questions

How long does ceramic coating last?

Ceramic coating can last anywhere from 2 to 5 years, depending on the product, maintenance, and environmental factors. Higher-end coatings may last longer with proper care.

How much does ceramic coating cost?

The cost of ceramic coating typically ranges from $500 to $2,000, depending on the brand, vehicle size, and professional application service. DIY kits can be less expensive, ranging from $50 to $150.

How much does ceramic coating cost for Tesla Model 3?

For a Tesla Model 3, ceramic coating typically costs between $800 to $1,500 for professional application, depending on the quality of the coating and the service provider.

Does ceramic coating protect against UV protection?

Yes, ceramic coatings provide UV protection by creating a protective layer that helps prevent oxidation and fading caused by the sun’s UV rays.

Does ceramic coating protect against rock chips?

Ceramic coating does not directly protect against rock chips. While it offers a layer of protection for the paint, it is not thick enough to prevent chips from stones or debris.

Does ceramic coating protect from scratches?

Ceramic coatings can provide some scratch resistance, but they do not make the surface completely scratch-proof. They can help prevent minor scratches by adding a hard protective layer.

Does ceramic coating protect from sun?

Yes, ceramic coating helps protect your vehicle's paint from the harmful effects of the sun by blocking UV rays, which can prevent fading and oxidation over time.

Does ceramic coating protect against salt?

Ceramic coating offers some protection against salt, particularly in terms of preventing salt buildup on the surface. However, it does not make the vehicle completely immune to salt damage, especially in harsh conditions.
